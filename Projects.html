Projects & Research

Selected academic and personal work.

Cost Optimization for Urban Road Networks

Objective: Evaluate lifecycle costs and identify cost-saving strategies for municipal road projects.

Method: Comparative analysis using value engineering and risk-adjusted budgeting.

Tools: Excel, Python (Pandas), cost estimation frameworks.

Outcome: Proposed a 12–18% reduction in lifecycle costs through material selection and phased maintenance.

Urban Data Systems for Waste Management

Objective: Use sensor data to optimize collection routes and reduce operational costs.

Method: Data cleaning, route modeling, and performance benchmarking.

Tools: Python, Pandas, GIS (placeholder), Excel dashboards.

Outcome: Demonstrated potential for 10% fuel savings and improved service reliability.

Affordable Housing Cost Benchmarking

Objective: Benchmark construction costs for affordable housing across regions.

Method: Data collection, normalization, and comparative analysis.

Tools: Excel, Python, visualization (placeholder).

Outcome: Identified key cost drivers and policy levers for affordability.
